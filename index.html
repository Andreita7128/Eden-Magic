¿What is it?

Magic Eden is a decentralized NFT marketplace built on the Solana blockchain. We can process over 65,000 transactions per second and have a block time of one second.

Launchpad function

Magic Eden's minting platform, which is also used as a research program, makes the creation and distribution of NFT an easy process and requires little or no technical knowledge.

Upcoming launches

Magic Eden tracks and vets the projects it features on the platform, also highlighting some of the most important upcoming launches. This can help investors save a lot of research work.

Stats
